**Sidereal does not collect personal data.**

This site is a static documentation site. There are no accounts, no contact forms, no cookies, and no analytics. Visiting this site does not result in any personal information being collected or stored by Primaris Tech LLC.

## What we do not collect

- No analytics or tracking (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no equivalent)
- No cookies of any kind
- No IP address logging
- No form submissions or user-generated content

## Hosting

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process request metadata (such as IP addresses) as part of normal network operations and DDoS protection. Cloudflare's privacy practices are governed by their own [privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## The software

Sidereal runs entirely within your own Kubernetes cluster. It makes no outbound connections to Primaris Tech LLC infrastructure. No telemetry, no phone-home, no license checks.

## Contact

Questions or concerns can be raised via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/primaris-tech/sidereal/issues).
